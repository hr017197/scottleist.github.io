Introduction

My name is Scott Leist, I am a Videographer and Video Editor. I have been in the digital media field for 5 years, working on projects such as music videos and tech YouTube videos. My passion for digital creation sparked when I was in high school as I began streaming on the platform Twitch.tv. During my time as a streamer, I fell in love with all aspects of social media creation such as photography, videography, and social media management.

When I started my career as an editor, I did freelance work for many creators. As I developed my skills within these jobs I was offered an opportunity to work on a tech/social media strategy YouTube channel “Senpai Gaming” and worked with the team there for 2 1/2 years exclusively. In March of 2023 I parted ways with the company to explore other creative endeavors. Since then, I have worked with other YouTube creators such as Hayden Hillier Smith, The Editing Podcast, Starter Story and more.

By combining my technical expertise and dedication to producing high-quality videos, I am committed to helping content creators reach their target audiences and achieve their vision. Contact me to discuss how I can contribute to your company's success.
