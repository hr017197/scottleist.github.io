Introduction

Scott Leist is a video editor, videographer, and digital media strategist with 8 years of hands on experience creating content across YouTube, social media, and branded digital platforms.

Scott’s passion for content creation started early and evolved into a career working alongside some of the most respected creators and brands in the space. His work spans high performing YouTube channels including KickinTheirBassTV, Hayden Hillier Smith, Starter Story, and The Editing Podcast, as well as numerous other creators and businesses.

Beyond editing and cinematography, Scott brings a strong understanding of the full content ecosystem, from creative direction and storytelling to brand deal negotiation, sponsorship strategy, and social media management. This allows him to not only produce polished, engaging videos, but also help creators and brands grow sustainably and monetize effectively.

Known for a detail driven approach and a deep respect for each creator’s voice, Scott focuses on creating content that feels authentic, intentional, and built to perform. His goal is simple: help great ideas reach the right audience, and make an impact when they do.
